# Superannuation (Super)

Superannuation is Australia's retirement savings system. Your employer pays a percentage of your wage into a super fund on top of your salary, called the super guarantee. That money is invested for you and grows until you retire.

You can usually choose your fund and the investment option inside it, such as a high growth or balanced option. Younger people often pick higher growth because they have decades for the market to recover from dips.

Watch the fees and check you do not have multiple funds quietly charging you. You can also make extra voluntary contributions, which can be tax effective. This is general information only, not personal financial advice.
