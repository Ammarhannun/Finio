# HECS-HELP student loans

HECS-HELP is the Australian government loan that covers university tuition. You only start repaying once your income passes a yearly threshold, and repayments come out automatically through the tax system as a percentage of your income.

HECS debt is indexed to inflation each year rather than charged normal interest, so the balance grows with the cost of living. Because the indexation rate is often lower than other debts, many people repay it slowly through their wages and put spare money toward higher cost goals first.

Tell your employer about your HECS debt so the right amount is withheld and you avoid a tax bill. This is general information only, not personal financial advice.
